    This District derives its name from the heritage city of Puri, one of the four pilgrimage centres of India. Covering an area of 3051 sq/kms, the District may be divided into -the Littoral tract and the Level alluvial tract.The population of the District is 15, 02, 682 (as per 2001 Census) and the density of population is 431 people per sq. km. SC population is 2, 73, 917 and ST is 4, 482.
